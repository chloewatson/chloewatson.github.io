About

Chloe Watson is an artist based in Sydney, Australia. She uses her own photographs and found images to create her collages.

Chloe is also in an ongoing digital collaboration with artist Tony Coleing - examples of this work can be found here.

Recent exhibitions:

'Techloony' - Utopia Art Sydney (November 2016, with Tony Coleing)

'Clay 3' - Utopia Art Sydney (February 2016)

'Looking Out, Looking In' - Gaffa Gallery (2015)

'Small Publishers' - Brenda May Gallery (2015)
